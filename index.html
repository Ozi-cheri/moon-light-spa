Welcome to Moon Light Spa

Step into a world of serenity and rediscover your inner peace at Moon Light Spa. Our journey began in 1995, with humble roots in the heart of Asia. We are honored to bring our rich heritage and timeless traditions to Germany, creating a sanctuary of calm and rejuvenation for our cherished guests.

A Journey Across Continents

In a quaint village nestled among the lush green hills of Thailand, a revered healer named Mei opened a small massage parlour that became renowned for its restorative powers. The whispers of Mei's miraculous touch spread far and wide, attracting travelers from distant lands seeking solace and healing.

As the years passed, Mei's wisdom and techniques were passed down through generations. By the early 1990s, her legacy reached Europe, finding its new home in Germany. Here, in a charming corner of the city, the essence of Mei's ancient practices was infused with modern comforts, giving birth to Moon Light Spa.

Our Philosophy

At Moon Light Spa, we honor the traditional Asian methods that have been cherished for centuries while embracing contemporary approaches to wellness. Our experienced therapists are skilled in a variety of techniques, ensuring that every massage is not only a therapeutic experience but also a journey through a rich cultural heritage.

Experience the Magic

From the moment you step through our doors, you are transported to a place where time slows down, and the stresses of the outside world fades away. Our tranquil environment, infused with the essence of ancient traditions, offers a unique escape designed to rejuvenate both body and spirit.

We invite you to experience the magic of Moon Light Spa, where every visit is a step into a world of peace, healing, and enchantment. Let us guide you on a journey of relaxation that transcends time and place.
